ABOUT ME

I'm a creative graphic designer with over eight years of experience in crafting innovative visuals for print media, websites, and social media. With expertise in design software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and CorelDRAW, I specialize in delivering high-quality designs with precise attention to detail and consistency under tight deadlines. Having completed a Professional Foundation course, I’m now advancing my skills in Front-End Development , focusing on technologies that merge design with web and mobile applications. This progression enables me to contribute to projects that blend creativity with technical expertise. Driven by passion, adaptability, and a commitment to staying ahead of industry trends, I’m eager to connect, collaborate, and contribute to exciting new projects that require a blend of design and technology.
